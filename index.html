Qing-Rui Wang (王晴睿)



I am an assistant professor at the Yau Mathematical Sciences Center, Tsinghua University since 2021.

Before that, I was a postdoctoral associate at the Department of Physics, Yale University (advised by Prof. Meng Cheng) and the Department of Physics, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (advised by Prof. Zheng-Cheng Gu). I obtained my Ph.D. in Physics from the Institute for Advanced Study, Tsinghua University in 2016 (supervised by Prof. Zheng-Yu Weng), and B.S. in Mathematics and Physics from the Department of Physics, Tsinghua University in 2011.

I am interested in mathematical physics and condensed matter physics, especially topological quantum matters and related mathematics.

Openings for PhD and Postdoc positions are available.
